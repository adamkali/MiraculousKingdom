Miraculous Kingdom
Welcome to the Miraculous Kingdom, a game of scumduggery, backstabbing, and conspiracy. You are a member of a royal council of the kingdom, and you are trying to influence the monarch to make decisions that will benefit you and your faction. Using your abilities and might, you will try to either take control of the kingdom, destroy it from within, or save it from the other players. If you have not played before, please read the rules before playing. Have fun!
